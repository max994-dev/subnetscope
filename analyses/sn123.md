# Subnet 123 — MANTIS (Financial Forecasting Signals)

> Analyzed: 2026-05-05 · easy-entry score at time of analysis: 45.2/100

## TL;DR

MANTIS is a **financial prediction signal subnet** — miners submit encrypted
probabilistic predictions on 37 assets across 5 challenge types (binary
direction, barrier hitting, volatility buckets, breakout detection, cross-
sectional ranking). The killer mechanic: **orthogonality is rewarded** — a
moderate-accuracy miner with uncorrelated predictions earns more than an
accurate miner whose signal is redundant with the crowd. Burn fee mid (~0.30 τ),
238 active miners. Moving toward real revenue via signal monetization.

## What it actually does

At each round:
1. Validators issue a prediction challenge for an asset (BTC, ETH, US stocks,
   commodities, forex — 37 assets total)
2. Miners compute a probabilistic prediction and **encrypt it** using
   time-lock encryption (Drand-based) — so no miner can see others' answers
   before submitting
3. After the lock period, validators decrypt all payloads and evaluate each
   miner's **marginal contribution** to the ensemble's overall accuracy

Challenge types miners must support:
- **Binary direction** — will asset go up or down over the horizon?
- **Barrier hitting** — will price cross a specific level?
- **Volatility buckets** — which volatility range will realized vol fall in?
- **Breakout detection** — will a breakout occur?
- **Cross-sectional ranking** — rank assets by expected relative performance

The subnet is actively monetizing: public signals are being traded and
alpha-token holders will receive revenue via buybacks/burns/TAO deposits.

## Reward structure

The key mechanic: **rewards orthogonality, not just accuracy**.

- A miner at 60% accuracy with a unique signal might outperform a miner at
  75% accuracy who's correlated with the top-50 miners
- This prevents winner-take-all consolidation and rewards genuine independent
  research

Rolling evaluation window with recency weighting.

## What miners run

- **Hardware:** CPU only (statistical/ML prediction models).
- **Stack:** Python, the MANTIS repo (`github.com/Barbariandev/MANTIS`).
  You implement a prediction model and the axon handles encrypted submission.
- **Competitive advantage:** Access to alternative data (on-chain flows,
  options market data, social sentiment) and low correlation with consensus.

## Realistic earnings

At ~36 τ/day across 238 miners:

| Approach | TAO/day | USD/day (TAO ≈ $250) |
|---|---|---|
| Naive momentum baseline | ~0.03–0.07 τ | $7–$17 |

Plus future revenue-sharing as signals are monetized commercially.

## Honest assessment

**Pros:**
- Orthogonality reward prevents the typical Bittensor race-to-copy-the-top
- 5 challenge types create diverse earning paths
- Revenue monetization roadmap is credible (publicly trading signals now)
- Time-lock encryption prevents free-riding on others' predictions

**Cons:**
- 0.30 τ burn fee
- 238 miners (crowded)
- Encrypted submission + time-lock adds implementation complexity
- 37 assets × 5 challenge types = your model must cover a lot of territory
