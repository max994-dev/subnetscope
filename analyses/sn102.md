# Subnet 102 — ConnitoAI / Vocence (Voice & Multimodal AI)

> Analyzed: 2026-05-05 · easy-entry score at time of analysis: 39.5/100

## TL;DR

Vocence (listed on-chain as ConnitoAI, SN102) is a **multimodal voice
intelligence subnet** — miners run voice synthesis, cloning, speech-to-text,
speech-to-speech, and text-to-music models. Mid burn fee (~0.085 τ), only
18 active miners, but **development is listed as inactive** (0 commits in
30 days) and health rank is #111/128. Highest-risk subnet on the recommended
list — could be abandoned, or could be in a quiet development phase. The
concept is sound, but do your research before investing.

## What it actually does

The subnet covers multimodal voice tasks:
- **Prompt-based TTS (PromptTTS)** — generate speech with style, emotion, and
  speaker characteristics controlled via text prompt
- **STT** — high-accuracy speech recognition (transcription)
- **Speech-to-Speech (STS)** — real-time voice conversion
- **Voice cloning** — replicate a speaker's voice from a short sample
- **Text-to-Music (TTM)** — generate music from text descriptions

Validators issue tasks and score miners on output quality using perceptual
metrics (MOS, speaker similarity, WER for STT).

## Reward structure

Proportional to quality scores. With 18 active miners the per-miner emission
is high (theoretically ~2 τ/day each), but inactive development raises
questions about whether validators are scoring consistently.

## What miners run

- **Hardware:** GPU strongly recommended. TTS/STS/voice cloning models range
  from 500 MB (Coqui) to 5 GB+ (XTTS, Bark). 8 GB VRAM minimum for
  competitive quality.
- **Stack:** Python, the Vocence repo (`github.com/vocence-bt/vocence`),
  HuggingFace models (Bark, XTTS-v2, Whisper, MusicGen).

## Realistic earnings

If validators are active and scoring properly:

| Setup | TAO/day | USD/day (TAO ≈ $250) |
|---|---|---|
| Baseline XTTS + Whisper | ~1–2 τ | $250–$500 |

But these are theoretical — 0 commits in 30 days is a red flag.

## Honest assessment

**Pros:**
- Only 18 active miners → high per-miner theoretical emission
- Interesting and commercially relevant (voice AI market is enormous)
- Mid burn fee not prohibitive

**Cons:**
- **0 commits in 30 days** — possible abandonment
- Health rank #111/128 — validator scoring may be unreliable
